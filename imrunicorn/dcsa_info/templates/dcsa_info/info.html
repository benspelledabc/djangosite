As of 3/31/2021 you use this form to request FOIA/PA info. FOIA is a query about someone else's info, PA is about your own info.

Fill it out, include photo copies of identification, etc etc. For the request itself, you may want to consider one of these that have example requests.

When you send in the request, you can send it snail mail (or certified mail) or even e-mail. With COVID in mind, the people working the ticket might be working from alternate locations or under a lower staff. Consider that when sending your request.

Privacy act requests, unlike FOIA requests, are not subject to the statutory 20 business day processing time. Requests are processed FIFO. First in, being when the responding agency receives the request. They are not processed based on ease of request.

As more info or guidance becomes available, we'll post it here to keep things easy to follow.
